Cookies Policy

Learn how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

Last Updated: January 28, 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help the website remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

In Short: We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms.

  • Purpose: Website functionality, security, and user session management
  • Examples: Login status, shopping cart, form data

2.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site.

  • Purpose: Analytics and website performance optimization
  • Examples: Google Analytics, page view tracking, bounce rates

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

  • Purpose: Remembering user preferences and settings
  • Examples: Language preferences, region settings, layout preferences

2.4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites.

  • Purpose: Marketing and advertising optimization
  • Examples: Retargeting cookies, social media pixels

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use services from third parties that set their own cookies on your device. These include:

  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site
  • Social Media: Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter for social sharing features
  • Payment Processors: Cookies from payment service providers

4. How to Control Cookies

4.1 Browser Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and data stored

4.2 Opt-Out Tools

For third-party advertising cookies, you can opt out through:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) opt-out: optout.aboutads.info
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) opt-out: optout.networkadvertising.org

5. Cookie Duration

Cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until deleted

6. Do Not Track Signals

Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals from browsers. However, you can control cookie usage through your browser settings as described above.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

About CharlesGT Laundry Services

Transforming laundry from a chore into a seamless, professional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, CharlesGT began with a simple mission: to provide busy professionals and families in Lagos with reliable, high-quality laundry services that save time and deliver exceptional results. What started as a small neighborhood service has grown into a trusted laundry partner for hundreds of satisfied customers across the city.

Our founder, Adeola Johnson, recognized the need for a laundry service that combined professional expertise with convenience. After years of watching friends and family struggle to balance work, family, and household chores, she created SparkleClean to offer a solution that delivers pristine laundry right to your doorstep.

Our Vision: To be Nigeria's most trusted and innovative laundry service, making clean clothes accessible to everyone while setting new standards in customer care and environmental responsibility.

Our Mission
